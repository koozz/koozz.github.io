NAME

koozz-games — games and interactive entertainment

SYNOPSIS

koozz --play [title]

DESCRIPTION

A lifelong gamer, starting from the Amstrad CPC era through to modern PC gaming. Enjoys co-op shooters, strategy games, and the occasional roguelike.

TITLES
Dungeon Defenders

Tower defense meets action RPG. Sank countless hours into building, upgrading, and perfecting defenses.

Mindustry

Factory building and tower defense. The automation and logistics puzzles keep pulling you back in.

Elden Ring

A breathtaking open world with punishing but rewarding combat. The art direction and landscapes are unmatched.

Path of Exile 2

Deep ARPG with endless build variety. Best enjoyed with a controller on the couch.

Deep Rock Galactic

Rock and Stone! Details coming soon. For Karl!
