NAME

koozz-contrib — open source contributions and community work

SYNOPSIS

koozz --contribute [project]

DESCRIPTION

Active contributor to open source projects and communities, with a focus on OSPO (Open Source Program Office) practices and infrastructure.

TODO GROUP

Member of the TODO Group, an open community of practitioners who aim to create and share knowledge about open source program management.

OSPO Book

Technical reviewer for the OSPO Book, a comprehensive guide on establishing and running Open Source Program Offices. The book covers strategy, governance, compliance, and community engagement.

Infrastructure Team

Member of the TODO Group infrastructure team, working on the tooling and systems that support the community.
